NAS IEMS

Integrated Engine Management System

Intelligent Engine Management for Professional UAV Platforms.

NAS IEMS is Nordic Advanced Systems' proprietary digital engine management platform, purpose-built for professional UAV propulsion systems — integrating Electronic Fuel Injection (EFI), Capacitor Discharge Ignition (CDI) and proprietary ECU technology into one intelligent control platform.

/ 01

Proprietary Technology

NAS IEMS is developed entirely around Nordic Advanced Systems' proprietary EFI, CDI and ECU hardware, embedded software and control strategies, protected by our own Intellectual Property (IP). Built on an NDAA-compliant architecture, the platform provides customers with a secure, scalable and future-ready engine management solution for professional UAV and defence applications.

/ 02

Flexible Integration

NAS IEMS is available both as part of a complete Nordic Advanced Systems propulsion package and as a standalone engine management solution for compatible third-party engines. Working closely with OEMs and UAV manufacturers, our engineering team supports platform integration, ECU calibration and engine mapping to ensure optimum performance on each individual propulsion system.

/ 03

Validation & Calibration

Every engine platform has unique operating characteristics. Using our dedicated engine test facilities, Nordic Advanced Systems performs platform-specific calibration, validation and performance optimization to deliver a fully integrated, mission-ready propulsion solution.

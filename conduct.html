/ 01

Ethical Business Conduct

At NAS (Nordic Advanced Systems), we are committed to conducting business with integrity, transparency, and responsibility. We follow all applicable laws and internal policies to ensure that we operate according to the highest ethical standards. We do not tolerate any form of bribery, corruption, facilitation payments, or improper advantage.

/ 02

Quality and Continuous Improvement

We operate under a self-developed and certified Quality Management System (QMS), which ensures that our products and services meet the highest quality standards. The QMS is based on continuous improvement, audits, and strict quality control. This ensures that we consistently deliver reliable and high-performing solutions to our partners.

/ 03

Data Protection and Confidentiality

We handle all data with great care and in compliance with applicable data protection regulations, including GDPR. Confidentiality is a fundamental principle at NAS, and we ensure that information from partners, employees, and stakeholders is treated with the highest level of security and discretion.

/ 04

Conflicts of Interest

Employees and representatives of NAS must avoid situations where personal interests conflict with company interests. Any potential conflict must be reported immediately so that an appropriate and transparent solution can be found.

/ 05

Work Environment, Diversity, and Respect

NAS strives to create an inclusive, safe, and respectful workplace for all employees. Discrimination, harassment, or bullying are not tolerated. We promote equal opportunities regardless of nationality, gender, age, or background. As an international company, we value the diversity of our global team and ensure that all employees are onboarded and employed in accordance with Danish law and international labor standards.

/ 06

International Trade Compliance

NAS complies with all applicable international trade laws, customs requirements, and sanctions regimes. We ensure that all production and trade activities are carried out in alignment with EU and local regulations in the countries where we operate.

/ 07

Responsible Supply Chain

We expect our suppliers and partners to comply with the same ethical, legal, and quality standards as NAS. We conduct due diligence in our supply chain to prevent corruption, human rights violations, and environmental harm, and we work only with responsible and reliable partners.

/ 08

Human Rights and Sustainability

NAS is committed to respecting internationally recognized human rights and labor standards, including the UN Guiding Principles on Business and Human Rights. We also seek to minimize our environmental footprint and continuously work towards sustainable solutions in both production and international trade.

/ 09

Financial Integrity

NAS is committed to financial transparency and integrity. We comply with all applicable anti-money laundering (AML) and financial regulations, ensuring that our business activities and relationships are conducted with accountability and trustworthiness.

/ 10

Compliance with Laws and Regulations

NAS complies with all relevant laws and regulations in the countries where we operate. This includes requirements for safety, health, and environmental protection. By adhering to both international and local standards, we ensure that our activities are fully aligned with responsible business practices.

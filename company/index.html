/ Company · NAS Profile 2026

Built on operational experience.

Nordic Advanced Systems develops and manufactures propulsion systems for UAV platforms — engineered for missions where propulsion must function correctly and predictably from start to completion.

/ 01 — About

Engines built for actual operations.

Nordic Advanced Systems is a Danish company that develops and manufactures propulsion systems for UAV platforms. The engines are used in operations where propulsion must function correctly and predictably throughout the mission. The focus is on stable operation, consistent quality, and known behavior under load.

In many UAV operations, it is essential that the propulsion system behaves consistently from unit to unit and can be produced and delivered in series with known characteristics. NAS develops engines based on this premise. The products are designed to provide predictable mechanical and thermal behavior, ensuring that overall platform performance is well understood in operational use.

/ 02 — Method

An industrial supplier with clear responsibility.

NAS is organized as an industrial supplier with established processes and clear responsibility.

The company draws on experience from operational military environments. This experience has helped shape the approach to function, testing, and accountability for what is delivered.

The working method is characterized by clear decision-making and clear ownership of both design and delivery.

/ 03 — Design Philosophy

Designed for real operating conditions.

The engines are developed based on actual operating conditions and known load profiles.

/ 01
Stable Rotational Speed
Throughout the mission, without unexpected drops or surges.
/ 02
Controlled Vibration
During operation, minimizing structural stress on the platform.
/ 03
Predictable Thermal Behavior
Until mission completion, with known limits and margins.
/ 04
Consistent Performance
Across series production, unit to unit.

Design choices are made to ensure stable and predictable function for the duration of the mission, based on known load profiles and operating conditions.

/ 04 — Production & Quality

Assembled and tested in Denmark.

All engines are assembled and tested in Denmark.

Each unit undergoes controlled cold-run and hot-run testing as well as mechanical and visual inspection. Documentation and unit-level traceability are provided.

Production is located within an existing industrial facility with established quality and process controls.

  • Cold-Run TestingMechanical verification under controlled conditions
  • Hot-Run TestingPerformance validation at operational load
  • Visual InspectionFinal review before unit release
